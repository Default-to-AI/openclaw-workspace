# Joseph Carlson: Meta and Amazon — Best Stocks 2026

**Platform:** YouTube | **Author:** Joseph Carlson | **Published:** 13-04-2026 | **Duration:** ~45 min

Joseph Carlson's top two stock picks for 2026: Meta and Amazon. He has $100K+ invested in each.

## Amazon Investment Thesis

### Key points
- **AWS AI demand**: Customer commitments for 2026 capex already secured. OpenAI partnership alone is $100B+.
- **Capex narrative**: Andy Jassey pushes back on skeptics — AWS capacity sold out 18+ months in advance
- **Chip business**: Graviton/Tranium custom chips — $20B run rate (now ~AMD scale), growing triple digits
- **Vertical integration**: Building own chips to reduce Nvidia dependency, save tens of billions in capex
- **Price target**: ~$598 (5-year, 19% CAGR) — currently ~$238

### Risks noted
- High capex = negative free cash flow through 2027 — short-term pain
- Skeptics cite "overbuilding" — Jassey argues customers are already committed

## Meta Investment Thesis

### Key points
- **Fast grower**: 22%+ revenue growth, expected 20-25% through 2026
- **Ad revenue surpassing Google**: E-arketer projects Meta will hit $243B vs Google's $240B in 2026
- **AI monetization**: Video generation tools at $10B run rate
- **Moat expanding**: 3.58B daily users, AI recommendation boosting watch time +30%
- **Price target**: ~$2,000 (5-year) — currently ~$550

### Risks noted
- Lawsuits over addictive features (youth use)
- $135B capex expected — high spend, but cash flow positive unlike Amazon
