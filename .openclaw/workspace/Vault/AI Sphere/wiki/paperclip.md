## What it is
PaperClip is presented as an open-source **agent orchestration framework** for building structured teams of AI agents (org chart, roles, accountability), with the ability to integrate multiple agent harnesses into a single organization.

## Notable points (from source)
- Emphasizes that agents work best with ongoing **human guidance**.
- Positions org structure, reporting, and accountability as the advantage over “solo agent” usage.
