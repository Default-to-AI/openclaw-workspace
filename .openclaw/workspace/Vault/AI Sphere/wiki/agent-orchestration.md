## Definition
Agent orchestration is the practice of coordinating multiple AI agents as a **team** (with roles, dependencies, reporting, and accountability) rather than relying on a single general agent.

## Patterns
- **Org chart / hierarchy** (CEO/CTO + departments)
- **Role specialization** (research, backtesting, risk, reporting)
- **Human-in-the-loop board** for taste, direction, and governance

## Why it matters
Orchestration aims to improve reliability and throughput by making responsibilities explicit and by creating structured feedback loops.
