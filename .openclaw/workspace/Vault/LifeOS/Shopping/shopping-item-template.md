> [!NOTE] Guide: How to use this template
> This template is designed for **deep-dive item research** before making big-ticket purchases. Instead of clogging up your `Pipeline.md` inbox, create a new note for the item and apply this template when it requires heavy consideration.
> 
> **Manual Workflow:**
> 1. Fill out the strict **Requirements** the product must meet.
> 2. Force yourself to find exactly **3 competing options** to avoid tunnel-vision.
> 3. Fill in the **Verdict** justifying *why* the winning option beat the other two.
> 4. Only once the Verdict is logically clear, fill in the **Buy** link.

> [!tip] 🤖 Agent Prompt (Autonomous Deep Dive)
> If Robert asks you to "Run a deep dive on [Item]":
> 1. **Ask Context First**: Ask Robert what his absolute "Must-Have" requirements are and if there is a strict budget limit. Do not guess these.
> 2. **Competitor Search**: Once requirements are given, autonomously scrape the web and populate `## Options (3)` with the original item PLUS two direct market competitors that perfectly match the requirements. Include comparative pricing.
> 3. **Draft the Verdict**: Provide a highly cynical, rational recommendation in `## Verdict` prioritizing durability and cost-to-value ratio. Highlight severe tradeoffs for each option.
