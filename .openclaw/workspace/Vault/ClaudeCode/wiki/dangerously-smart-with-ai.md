# Summary: Give Me 14 Minutes and I'll Make You Dangerously Smart with AI

Dan Martell argues the real edge with AI is not saving time but learning faster and making better decisions.

## Key Ideas

1. **AI as brain upgrade, not just time saver.** The competitive advantage is using AI to upgrade inputs (what you learn), stress-test outputs (decisions), and build repeatable operating systems.
2. **Systems over prompts.** Build AI into learning loops, team execution, and decision pressure-testing as recurring workflows, not one-off prompt interactions.
3. **Three application areas:**
   - Learning loops (faster knowledge acquisition)
   - Team execution (delegation and coordination)
   - Decision pressure-testing (challenge assumptions before committing)
