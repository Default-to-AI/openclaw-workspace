# Axe Capital — RACI (Core Processes)

Roles (edit to match reality):
- **CIO/PM** (decision owner)
- **Analyst**
- **Trader**
- **Risk** (independent)
- **Compliance**
- **Ops/MO** (middle office)
- **Finance/Controller**
- **Tech/Data**
- **IR**

## 1) Idea intake → research → IC decision
| Activity | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Idea intake + initial screen | Analyst | CIO/PM | Risk | Trader, Ops |
| Full research + model + memo draft | Analyst | CIO/PM | Risk, Compliance | Trader, Ops |
| IC pre-read distribution | Analyst | CIO/PM | Compliance | All |
| IC discussion + decision | CIO/PM | CIO/PM | Risk, Compliance | Ops, Finance |
| Decision log update | Analyst | CIO/PM | Risk | All |

## 2) Pre-trade checks and execution
| Activity | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Pre-trade compliance check (restricted list, MNPI) | Compliance | Compliance | CIO/PM | Trader |
| Pre-trade risk check (limits, liquidity) | Risk | Risk | CIO/PM | Trader |
| Order construction (size, route, urgency) | Trader | CIO/PM | Risk | Ops |
| Best execution + fills | Trader | CIO/PM | Risk | Ops |
| Trade capture (OMS/EMS) | Trader | CIO/PM | Ops | Finance |

## 3) Post-trade operations and controls
| Activity | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Confirmations/settlement | Ops/MO | Ops/MO | Trader | Finance |
| Reconciliations (positions/cash) | Ops/MO | Ops/MO | Finance | CIO/PM, Risk |
| Corporate actions processing | Ops/MO | Ops/MO | Finance | CIO/PM |
| Valuation checks / price challenges | Finance/Controller | Finance/Controller | Ops/MO, Risk | CIO/PM |

## 4) Risk management
| Activity | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Daily risk report | Risk | Risk | CIO/PM | Trader, Ops |
| Limit monitoring and breaches | Risk | Risk | CIO/PM, Compliance | Ops, Finance |
| Stress testing / scenarios | Risk | Risk | CIO/PM | All |
| Drawdown protocol activation | CIO/PM | CIO/PM | Risk, Compliance | IR, Ops, Finance |

## 5) Investor reporting
| Activity | R | A | C | I |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Monthly letter + factsheet | IR | CIO/PM | Risk, Finance, Compliance | Ops |
| DDQs / due diligence | IR | CIO/PM | Compliance, Ops, Risk | Finance |
| Performance attribution summary | Risk or Analyst | CIO/PM | Finance | IR |
