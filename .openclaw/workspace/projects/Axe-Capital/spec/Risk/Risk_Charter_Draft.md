# Axe Capital — Risk Charter (Draft)

## 1) Objective
Deliver strong risk-adjusted returns by enforcing a repeatable risk process, explicit limits, and escalation paths.

## 2) Risk philosophy
- **Survival first**: avoid forced deleveraging and permanent impairment.
- **Know your drivers**: risk is factor/cluster exposure, not ticker count.
- **Liquidity is a risk factor**: size to liquidity, not conviction.
- **Documented discretion**: exceptions are allowed, but logged, time-boxed, and reviewed.

## 3) Risk governance and independence
- Risk provides an independent view, sets monitoring, and can escalate breaches.
- Final portfolio decisions remain with CIO/PM, except where kill-switch triggers mandate de-risking.

## 5) Monitoring
- Daily: exposures, top risk contributors, liquidity, limits, PnL explain
- Weekly: stress tests, scenario shocks, crowdedness review
- Monthly: attribution, limit calibration, process review

## 6) Breach escalation
- **Soft breach**: notify CIO/PM + plan within 24h
- **Hard breach**: immediate huddle, documented action, timeline to compliance
- **Kill-switch**: automatic de-risking actions if triggered

## 7) Drawdown protocol
- Thresholds: warning / action / stop
- Actions: reduce gross, cut tails, raise cash, reduce correlated exposures
- Post-mortem required after recovery or stop

## 8) Conflicts and MNPI
- MNPI policy, restricted list, personal trading restrictions, outside activities disclosure.

## 9) Documentation
- Decision log must include: thesis, size, risks, kill criteria, and review date.
