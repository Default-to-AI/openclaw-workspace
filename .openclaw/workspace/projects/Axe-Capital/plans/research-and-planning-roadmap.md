# Research & Planning Roadmap (Hedge-Fund Style Agent Org)

## Objective
Before building agents, we define the hedge-fund operating model: roles, responsibilities, handoffs, cadence, and artifacts.

## Phase 0 — Strategy constraints (you)
- manual execution
- paper-first
- facts-only memos + explicit assumptions

## Phase 1 — Research: how hedge funds *actually* operate
### Questions to answer
- What is the minimal viable HF org for a solo PM?
- What does an analyst do daily vs in earnings season?
- What is the "IC packet" (investment committee memo) structure?
- Where does risk have veto, and what is the risk charter?
