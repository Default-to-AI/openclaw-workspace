# Axe Capital — Cadence (Meetings, Reports, and Decision Rhythm)

## Daily (market days)
- **08:30** Risk + positioning snapshot (gross/net, factors, concentrations, liquidity buckets)
- **09:00** Catalyst board: today/this-week events, earnings, macro prints, idiosyncratic risks
- **16:15** EOD PnL + risk check, exceptions, reconciliations status
- **Continuous**: breach alerts (limits, news), execution notes, decision log updates

## Weekly
- **Portfolio Review (60–90m)**
  - What changed, what worked, what broke
  - Adds/reduces/exits, top risk contributors, crowdedness
- **Risk Deep Dive (45–60m)**
  - Factor/cluster moves, scenario shocks, liquidity, tail hedges
- **Research Pipeline (45m)**
  - New ideas, memos in progress, upcoming catalysts

## Monthly
- **Performance + Attribution (60–90m)**: winners/losers, factor attribution, thesis tracking
- **Limits and Leverage Review (30m)**: adjust budgets, review breaches and exceptions
- **Ops/Compliance Review (30m)**: errors, near misses, surveillance flags, process updates

## Quarterly (IC-style governance cadence)
Institutional ICs often run on a **quarterly routine** with disciplined agendas focused on:
1) performance assessment vs targets/benchmarks,
2) macro assessment and tactical moves,
3) compliance with investment policy (risk, liquidity, allocation, sizing),
4) pressure-testing internal/advisor process.
(Partners Capital, 2026.)

- **Quarterly Strategy Day (half-day)**: regime map, scenario plan, risk budget refresh
- **Investor call / update** (if applicable)

## Ad-hoc (event-driven)
- **Crisis huddle (15m)**: drawdown threshold hit, funding/liquidity events, major news
- **IC exception vote**: limit override, instrument change, leverage change
